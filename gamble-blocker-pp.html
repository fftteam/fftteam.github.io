Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 14, 2026

GambleBlocker ("we", "our", or "the app") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the GambleBlocker mobile application.

1. Overview

GambleBlocker is a self-help app designed to support people who want to stop or reduce gambling. The app helps users track their progress, reflect on their journey, and stay motivated.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and keeping your personal information secure.

2. Information We Collect

GambleBlocker is designed to minimize data collection.

Most information entered into the app — including streak data, mood check-ins, journal entries, achievements, and motivation notes — is stored locally on your device.

We do not collect personally identifiable information unless explicitly stated in future updates.

3. Data Stored on Your Device

The following types of information may be stored locally on your device to provide app functionality:

  • Streak tracking data
  • Estimated money saved calculations
  • Daily mood check-ins
  • Journal entries
  • Milestone achievements
  • Your personal motivation message
  • App PIN lock settings

This information remains on your device unless you choose to delete the app or clear the data.

4. How Your Information Is Used

Your information is used only to provide the core features of the app, including:

  • Tracking your gambling-free streak
  • Displaying progress and achievements
  • Calculating estimated money saved
  • Recording mood check-ins and personal reflections
  • Displaying motivational reminders

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.

5. Device Permissions

GambleBlocker may provide guidance on using built-in device tools such as Screen Time to block gambling apps and websites. These settings are controlled by your device and are not managed by GambleBlocker itself.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information. Because most data is stored locally on your device, you maintain control over your information.

The optional PIN lock feature helps prevent unauthorized access to the app on your device.

7. Children's Privacy

GambleBlocker is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected by changing the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
